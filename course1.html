Best Restaurants in Oakland

  • Brotzeit Lokal - Experience authentic Bavarian cuisine with hearty dishes like pretzels, sausages, and schnitzel, paired with a wide selection of German beers.
  • Homeroom - Specializing in creative mac 'n' cheese, this cozy eatery is perfect for comfort food lovers of all ages.
  • Pizzaiolo - Known for its wood-fired pizzas and seasonal menu, this restaurant offers a delightful dining experience in a vibrant atmosphere.
  • Commis - A Michelin-starred gem offering innovative tasting menus in an intimate setting, perfect for a fine dining experience.
  • Hawker Fare - Enjoy vibrant Southeast Asian street food, featuring bold flavors and communal dining in a lively atmosphere.
