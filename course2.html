Best Places to Visit in Oakland

  • Redwood Regional Park - A sanctuary of coast redwoods, the tallest living things in the world, with hiking and horseback riding trails.
  • Oakland Museum of California - Explore California art, history, and natural sciences at one of the Bay Area's top-rated museums.
  • Children's Fairyland - A magical outdoor playground for young children, where they can create, imagine, play, and learn.
  • Joaquin Miller Park - Enjoy diverse terrain and stunning views of the bay and San Francisco from this lovely urban park.
  • Oakland Zoo - Home to over 850 animals, offering wildlife experiences across the African Savanna and California Trail.
